Virtual Course Minimum Yardage

For all virtual league events, players must play from the Men's tees, unless the total course yardage is less than 6,000 yards. In such cases, players are required to select the next available set of tees further back to ensure appropriate difficulty. Note: Courses with a total yardage of less than 6,000 yards are not eligible for official league play.
